Craft campaigns</h2><p>The next step is to create the campaign content. Some tips for writing effective campaign messages are:</p><p><strong>1. You can do this by analyzing past campaigns to find optimal timing.</p><p><strong>Tip</strong>: Instead of analyzing data manually, use a marketing automation platform with AI capabilities.
