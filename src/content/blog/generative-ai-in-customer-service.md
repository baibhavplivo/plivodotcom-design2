It can create new content, including text, images, and music decoding patterns, making customer interactions more personalized and dynamic.</p><p>This is done by learning from patterns in existing data and using that information to create contextually relevant answers on the spot.</p><h2>How does generative AI differ from conversational AI?</h2><p>To efficiently leverage the power of conversational and generative AI, you first have to understand their unique qualities.</p><p>Conversational AI, as the name suggests, works best for on-the-spot talks, while generative AI’s specializes in generating original content from prompts given by humans.</p><table>  <tbody><tr>   <td><strong>Basis</strong>   </td>   <td><strong>Conversational AI</strong>   </td>   <td><strong>Generative AI</strong>   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td>Core function   </td>   <td>Interactively communicates to handle predefined tasks like answering FAQs    </td>   <td>Creates new and unique content dynamically   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td>Technology   </td>   <td>Uses rule-based systems and Natural Language Understanding (NLU)   </td>   <td>Powered by advanced language models such as GPT to generate real-time responses   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td>Primary applications   </td>   <td>Customer support, educational bots, virtual assistants   </td>   <td>Content generation, media creation and design, data analysis   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td>Personalization   </td>   <td>Limited customization based on customer data   </td>   <td>Deep personalization by generating tailored responses   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td>Examples   </td>   <td>Chatbots, Siri, Alexa   </td>   <td>ChatGPT, Claude, Copy.ai   </td>  </tr>  <tr>   <td>Best for   </td>   <td>Straightforward, transactional tasks   </td>   <td>Creative or nuanced tasks   </td>  </tr></tbody></table><h2>12 use cases of generative AI in customer service&nbsp;</h2><p>When we think of generative AI for customer service, we generally think of chatbots and instant answers. It can then suggest personalized resolution suggestions or escalate to a human agent by analyzing the severity of the issue.	</p><h3>9.Immersive training</h3><p>Businesses can incorporate Gen AI to refine training programs and offer instant and accurate customer service.
