<p>A WhatsApp Business app gives you the power to create a virtual shopping experience for your customers that is as engaging and interactive as an in-store visit.</p><p>Through WhatsApp, customers can connect instantly, get their questions answered in real time, and browse products through the convenience of one of the world’s most popular apps.&nbsp;</p><p>It’s easy to use the WhatsApp Business app or the WhatsApp Business platform to create a WhatsApp Business account. Follow the steps in this guide to get started.&nbsp;</p><h2>The WhatsApp Business ecosystem: App, Account, and Platform</h2><ul><li>WhatsApp Business account: You need a WhatsApp Business app to use the app or the platform. The account grants you access to business-specific tools, such as customer support tools and business analytics.</li></ul><ul><li>WhatsApp Business app: It's designed for small businesses to manage customer interactions. It lets you set up a business profile, respond to customer messages, and automate responses to increase engagement.&nbsp;</li></ul><ul><li>WhatsApp Business Platform: It's a comprehensive solution for medium to large-scale businesses. It offers advanced features like bulk messaging, integration with CRM systems, and automated workflows, to manage and scale communications.&nbsp;</li></ul><h2>Create a WhatsApp Business account using the WhatsApp Business app&nbsp;</h2><p>A simple seven-step process is all it takes to set up the app with a WhatsApp Business account.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><h3>1. Register or use an existing number to create a WhatsApp Business Account</h3><p>Select the phone number you want to sync your WhatsApp Business account.&nbsp;</p><p>There are two options:</p><ol><li>Convert an existing phone number&nbsp;&nbsp;</li><li>Register for a new phone number&nbsp;</li></ol><figure style="max-width:850px" data-rt-max-width="850px"><div><img src="/images/blog/6704e76b1946b9303f94e5c6_AD_4nXdWlcGGBZDPcCeuLU0tRIJbN746jCYdkRQIYyagwoRooYs_My0-642de140.jpeg" width="auto" height="auto" alt="" loading="auto"></div></figure><p>Enter your phone number and select your country from the drop-down list. Verify your WhatsApp Business phone number</h3><p>Follow these instructions to complete your Business account verification.&nbsp;</p><ul><li><strong>Tap done: </strong>You’ll receive a 6-digit registration code via SMS. If you prefer voice calling, tap “Call me” to receive the code through an automated phone call.</li><li><strong>Enter code: </strong>Input the code you received via SMS or call in the verification field.&nbsp;</li><li><strong>Troubleshoot: </strong>If you don’t receive the code within a few minutes, tap “Resend code” or “Call me” again. Double-check if you’ve entered the correct phone number. Convert WhatsApp Messenger to WhatsApp Business (only if applicable)</h3><p>It is possible to transition your personal WhatsApp number to a WhatsApp Business account. Once you’ve verified your phone number, prompts will ask if you would like to:</p><ul><li><strong>Transfer data: </strong>Transfer all your data to the new WhatsApp Business profile, including images, videos, chat messages, and contact numbers by granting permission to complete the transfer.&nbsp;</li><li><strong>Verify: </strong>Enter the verification code if you’ve enabled two-factor authentication.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li></ul><p>Please note that the transfer process might take some time, depending on the size of your media files.&nbsp;</p><h3>6. Grant permission for WhatsApp Business to access files</h3><p>A prompt will ask for sharing permission with WhatsApp. Allow the WhatsApp Business app access to your contacts and media files to facilitate better customer connections.&nbsp;</p><h3>7. The additional information you share could include:&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><ul><li><strong>Business profile: </strong>Your operating hours, contact email address, and website URL.</li><li><strong>WhatsApp Business catalog: </strong>Products/services with optional details like price, description, website link, and product code.</li></ul><ul><li><strong>Messaging tools</strong>: Automated responses that handle common queries, provide instant replies, and ensure timely responses, making interactions more efficient.&nbsp;</li></ul><p><a href="https://faq.whatsapp.com/665179381840568/?cms_platform=web">Update your WhatsApp Business profile</a> regularly to reflect changes in your business information.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><p><strong><em>Pro tip: </em></strong><em>Keep your WhatsApp Business profile complete to inform your customers about your business and reduce recurring queries regarding your business hours, product images, etc. </em>Here’s how to <a href="https://faq.whatsapp.com/2565868990219715/?cms_platform=android">set an away message</a> in the WhatsApp Business app.&nbsp;</p><h2>Create a WhatsApp Business account using the WhatsApp Business Platform&nbsp;</h2><p>To get started, you have two options:</p><ul><li>Create a WhatsApp Business Account (WABA) on your own</li><li>Get help from a Business Solution Partner (BSP)</li></ul><p>The steps out here are for those who work with a BSP.
