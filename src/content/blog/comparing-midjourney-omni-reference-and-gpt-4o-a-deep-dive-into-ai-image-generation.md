This capability particularly benefits artists and designers who want to explore different contexts for their subjects without losing the essence of the original image.</p><p>The Omni-Reference feature includes a strength slider, enabling users to adjust image consistency. Higher values ensure more consistency with the reference image, while lower values allow for greater variation and creativity.
