Unified dashboard</h3><p>Look for a unified dashboard when choosing omnichannel support software. Cross-channel integration</h3><p>Your customers might start a query on chat, move to email, and then finalize the conversation over the phone. Ticketing and case management</h3><p>Seek robust ticketing and case management features in your omnichannel software.
