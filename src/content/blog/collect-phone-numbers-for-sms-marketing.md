You can add a small checkbox on the checkout page and a small note highlighting the benefits of signing up for the SMS list. Having these dedicated numbers brings more trust in customers.&nbsp;</p><p>RIPit, a performance gear brand, promoted its SMS marketing deals on social media. Get opt-in via website chat</h3><p>Use website chat as a medium to sign up for SMS marketing by sending a quick note like below:</p><p>"Before you go, join our SMS list for exclusive offers and early access to the latest collection! Enter your phone number, and we'll send you a special discount code."</p><h2>6 Best practices to get more opt-in from customers</h2><p>Follow the 6 best practices below to get maximum engagement from customers:</p><h3>1. Always ask permission before adding to the SMS list</h3><p>Never add any customer number to your SMS list without seeking permission. It looks like spam and increases your chances of being blocked. Use any of the techniques mentioned above to get customers' opt-in.</p><h3>2. Keep the opt-in instructions simple</h3><p>Make it easy for customers to sign up for your list.
