These templates are highly restricted and must adhere to the following rules:</p><ul><li>Use WhatsApp's pre-approved templates so you can add security warnings or expiration reminders.</li><li>Include an OTP button (either a copy-code or one-tap option).</li><li>Follow content restrictions: no links, media, or emojis, and length restrictions apply to certain parts of the message.</li></ul><h2>Tips &amp; best practices to create message templates</h2><p>Effective WhatsApp templates require more than just good copy — they must adhere to WhatsApp’s guidelines and meet strategic needs.&nbsp;</p><p>Here's how to make sure your templates hit the mark.</p><h3>Be clear and concise</h3><p>Prioritize clarity and conciseness above all else. Your messages should be easy to understand, even for someone unfamiliar with your industry or product.</p><p>You can use emojis to make your messages more fun, but don't go overboard. One or two is usually enough; too many can look unprofessional.</p><p>If you want your customers to take action, be explicit. Use clear, direct calls to action like “Reply YES” or “Tap to learn more.”</p><h3>Name your message templates</h3><p>Stick to short, descriptive names that are easy to remember and understand. Keep the name concise, ideally under 512 characters.</p><p>Use only lowercase letters, numbers, and underscores, and avoid spaces, special characters, or emojis. For example, <em>appointment_reminder</em> and <em>shipping_update </em>are good names because they contain acceptable characters.</p><p>On the other hand, <em>Appointment_reminder</em> and <em>re$et-password</em> are not good names because they contain uppercase letters and special characters.</p><h3>Format the template properly</h3><p>Use proper punctuation — commas, periods, question marks — so your message is easy to read. Add some space between lines of text. This makes your message less overwhelming, especially on mobile devices.</p><p>And before submitting your template for approval, double-check that everything is clear and that all variables, such as placeholders, are correctly formatted.</p><h3>Understand your customer's journey</h3><p>Consider how your customers interact with your brand.What information do they need at each step?Create messages that give them exactly what they need, when they need it.</p><p>For example, send an order confirmation after they buy something or an appointment reminder before their visit.&nbsp;</p><p>Always ensure you have their permission before sending messages, and provide an easy opt-out option.</p><h3>A/B test your templates</h3><p>Try out different message copy and calls to action to see what works best for your customers.</p><p>Consider sending different versions of your message to a small group and see which version gets a better response. This information helps you fine-tune messages for best results.</p><h2><sub>Tips to getting your Whatsapp template approved</sub></h2><p>When creating a template for approval, keep these guidelines in mind:</p><ul><li><strong>Adhere to the rules:</strong> Make sure your templates comply with the <a href="https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/business-policy/">WhatsApp Business Policy</a> and <a href="https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/commerce-policy/">Commerce Policy</a>.</li><li><strong>Choose the right message type:</strong> Understand the difference between session messages and templates. Make sure you're using the right one for your purpose.</li><li><strong>Number your placeholders:</strong> If you're using {{1}}, {{2}}, etc., make sure they're in order. Skipping numbers could lead to rejection.</li><li><strong>Avoid sensitive info requests:</strong> Don’t request sensitive personal information like full bank account numbers or ID numbers.</li><li><strong>Be respectful:</strong> Use polite, non-threatening language.</li><li><strong>Keep it unique:</strong> Every template you submit needs to be original. Duplicate templates are likely to be rejected.</li><li><strong>Stay in the loop:</strong> WhatsApp's policies can change, so always stay informed and keep your templates compliant.</li></ul><h2>Automate your WhatsApp communication with Plivo</h2><p>Plivo offers a robust, enterprise-grade communication platform to ensure your messages reach customers reliably, even at high volumes.
