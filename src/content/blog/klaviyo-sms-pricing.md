<p>Recently, Klaviyo made a change in its pricing structure. Previously, charges were based on the volume of emails or SMS messages sent. But now, you have to pay based on the number of contacts in your email or SMS list. For businesses with larger lists, this could mean a big price jump.&nbsp;</p><p>Additionally, Klaviyo offers a tiered pricing system. For instance, you'll pay a flat fee of $60 for 1,001-1,500 active profiles. This means whether you have 1,200 or 1,500 contacts, the cost remains the same.&nbsp;</p><p>The pricing structure can feel unfair for smaller and mid-size businesses with growing or fluctuating contact lists.
