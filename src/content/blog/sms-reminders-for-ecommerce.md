<p>You can make countless changes on product pages to optimize your conversion rate. Yet often, all it needs is a simple nudge—a quick reminder text to customers that the product they left in the cart is about to go out of stock or the sale they were waiting for is now live.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><figure><div><img src="/images/blog/67fe0a46fdf01b69758d0127_67b587e1f91dd09cf260ac13_AD_4nXf5ujEQtpwn5Bb8p4IqVsITmQ-d09a5a74.png" alt="" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div><figcaption>Importance of nudge marketing | Source: <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/posts/oksana-tsvigun_serie-9nudging-activity-7150516627896201217-LMLV">LinkedIn</a></figcaption></figure><p><a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/oksana-tsvigun/">Oksana Tsvigun</a>, an eCommerce expert, says, "<em>When executed effectively, nudge marketing can significantly influence purchase behavior across various channels.</em>"</p><p>In this post, we will explore the type of SMS reminders eCommerce businesses can send and how they can send.&nbsp;</p><h2><strong>Types of SMS reminders eCommerce businesses can send</strong></h2><p>Here are some of the must-have SMS reminders for your online store:</p><h3><strong>1. Abandoned cart SMS reminder</strong></h3><p>Abandoned cart reminders are a no-nonsense strategy for winning back sales from customers who left their shopping cart in the middle for some reason.&nbsp;</p><p>Sending quick abandoned cart reminder texts is effective as they are direct and encourage immediate action. Studies show a well-crafted text can recover up to <a href="https://www.slicktext.com/blog/2019/08/data-recover-abandoned-carts/">58% of these carts</a>, potentially bringing back nearly two out of three lost sales. This means many customers who abandon their carts may return after a text reminder.</p><p>Further,<a href="https://www.validity.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/The-State-of-SMS-Marketing-in-2023.pdf"> high open rates of SMS</a> pose another promising metric.</p><h4><strong>Sample abandoned cart SMS reminder:</strong></h4><blockquote>Hi [Customer Name], [Product name] is waiting in your cart. Complete the purchase now and get free shipping! [Link to Cart]</blockquote><h3><strong>2. Payment SMS reminder</strong></h3><p>Around<a href="https://www.pymnts.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/PYMNTS-Fraud-Management-False-Declines-and-Improved-Profitability-November-2023.pdf"> 11% of online transactions</a> among eCommerce sites failed in 2023. This failure can significantly impact revenue as customers usually step away from the site after a failure, thinking to try later, and may ultimately forget about the item itself.&nbsp;</p><p>A quick reminder text nudges them to complete the purchase in such cases.</p><h4><strong>Sample payment SMS reminder:</strong></h4><blockquote>Hi [Customer Name], There was a technical glitch when completing your order payment. Please retry payment now : [Link to payment page].</blockquote><h3><strong>3. Product restock SMS reminder</strong></h3><p>Product restock reminders are another great way to increase sales by sending messages to customers who previously expressed interest in an out-of-stock product. It's a proven tactic to regain lost sales due to supply chain issues.&nbsp;</p><h4><strong>Sample product restock SMS reminder:</strong></h4><blockquote>Hi [Customer Name], The [Product Name] you were waiting for is back in stock. Order now: [Link to Product Page]</blockquote><h3><strong>4. Order confirmation SMS reminder</strong></h3><p>Order confirmation reminders help improve customer experience and gain their trust. It ensures customers get an instant confirmation message with a hint of shipping and further tracking details.&nbsp;</p><p>Customers are never left wondering whether the payment they just made resulted in a successful order or not.&nbsp;</p><h4><strong>Sample order confirmation SMS reminder:</strong></h4><blockquote>Hi [Customer name], Thank you for purchasing from [Site name], Your order [Order number] is confirmed. We will send a tracking link as soon as the order is shipped.</blockquote><h3><strong>5. Shipping/Delivery SMS reminder</strong></h3><p><a href="https://www.dhl.com/content/dam/dhl/local/global/dhl-ecommerce/documents/pdf/g0-ecs-insights-delivery-and-returns-report.pdf">83% of shoppers look for text updates</a> about their order shipping status. Timely shipping updates are crucial for maintaining customer satisfaction.&nbsp;</p><p>A short text message with a tracking link and delivery details goes a long way to win customer trust in the current cluttered market.&nbsp;</p><h4><strong>Sample shipping/delivery SMS reminder:</strong></h4><blockquote>Hey [Customer name]: Wohooo! Your order [Order Id] is on the way and expected to arrive by [Tentative delivery date]. Track it here: [Shipping tracking link]</blockquote><h3><strong>6. Feedback/Review SMS reminder</strong></h3><p><a href="https://www.powerreviews.com/power-of-reviews-2023/">77% of shoppers</a> specifically seek out websites with ratings and reviews. Having reviews on the website brings more credibility to your store.&nbsp;</p><p>Therefore, feedback/review requests are messages customers get after they have received the product. The message contains a link to drop a review on the product/delivery experience.&nbsp;</p><h4><strong>Sample feedback/review SMS reminder:</strong></h4><blockquote>Hi [Customer], We hope you are loving your recent purchase. Tap this link [Review link] to drop a review and collect loyalty points</blockquote><h3><strong>7. Event/Sale SMS reminder</strong></h3><p>Event/sale SMS reminders are text messages sent to customers to notify them about upcoming events, flash sales, holiday promotions, or special offers.&nbsp;</p><p>These reminders are pivotal in increasing the engagement of these promotions and generating more sales.&nbsp;</p><h4><strong>Sample event/sale SMS reminder:</strong></h4><blockquote>[Holiday name] Offers Alert! Exciting sales start at [Promotion start date]. Get up to 50% off your favorite products. Start wishlisting now.&nbsp;</blockquote><h3><strong>8. VIP/Exclusive offer SMS reminder</strong></h3><p>VIP/Exclusive offers are usually for customers who are part of loyalty programs. These are text messages containing special discounts or early access to some products.&nbsp;</p><h4><strong>Sample VIP/Exclusive offer SMS reminder:</strong></h4><blockquote>Hey [Customer name], [Brand name] Summer collection is going to be live soon. As a [Loyalty program ] member, you can access it early here [Collection link]. Get your hands on it before it goes live.</blockquote><h3><strong>9. Subscription renewal SMS reminder</strong></h3><p>Some eCommerce brands also offer subscriptions in return for exclusive perks.&nbsp;</p><p>One typical example is the famous <a href="https://www.amazon.in/amazonprime">Amazon Prime program</a>, which provides customers with free shipping. If you offer such subscriptions, you can send text message reminders to customers for renewals.&nbsp;</p><h4><strong>&nbsp;Sample Subscription renewal SMS reminder:</strong></h4><blockquote>Hey [Customer name], Your [Subscription program] subscription is about to expire. Renew it now to keep enjoying the perks-&gt; [Payment link]</blockquote><h2><strong>How to send reminder SMS to eCommerce consumers</strong></h2><p>For brands seeking to maximize customer engagement, follow this six-step process to create a robust SMS reminder system:</p><h3><strong>1. Boost sales and revenue</strong></h3><p>Most types of reminders (abandoned cart, product restock, offers, etc.) are a simple way to gain sales. Encourage reviews and feedback</strong></h3><p>Sending reminder texts gives quick nudges to customers to drop feedback.
