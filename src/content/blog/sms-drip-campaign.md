<p>There are <a href="https://www.digitalcommerce360.com/2023/09/19/with-14-million-ecommerce-sites-the-u-s-is-atop-the-online-world/#:~:text=Between%202019%20and%202023%2C%20the,more%20than%20any%20other%20country.">14 million eCommerce stores in the US</a> alone fighting for consumer attention. Amidst this competition, <a href="https://www.bynder.com/en/blog/delivering-an-omnichannel-marketing-strategy/">according to a recent survey conducted by Bynder</a>, the average marketing strategy requires approximately six touchpoints with consumers to make a sale—social media, online advertising, websites, newsletters, email marketing, and product catalogs.&nbsp;</p><p>But how do you ensure consistent, impactful engagement without overwhelming your audience?</p><p>The answer lies in SMS drip campaigns. These strategically-timed text messages help you guide consumers through their journey, from discovery to decision-making, while building lasting relationships.</p><p>In this article, we explore the key components of SMS drip campaigns, share real-world examples, and provide actionable steps to create campaigns that maximize your ROI and revenue.<br></p><h2>What is an SMS drip campaign?&nbsp;</h2><p>SMS drip campaigns are a series of pre-automated messages that are sent to your subscribers either at different intervals or in response to their actions. It is a popular marketing strategy to keep consumers engaged and boost sales.&nbsp;</p><p>Here’s the revised section with benefits highlighted, examples included, and statistics with sources:</p><h3>Key benefits of SMS drip campaigns</h3><p>SMS drip campaigns offer several significant benefits that make them an essential part of modern marketing strategies:</p><ol><li><strong>High engagement rates</strong>: SMS messages boast an open rate of approximately 98%, with <a href="https://textellent.com/blog/sms-marketing-statistics/#:~:text=95%25%20of%20SMS%20messages%20are%20read%20within%20just%20three%20minutes,updates%20or%20time%2Dsensitive%20offers.">95% of texts read and responded</a> to within three minutes of delivery.<br></li><li><strong>Improved conversion rates</strong>: The immediacy and high engagement of SMS contribute to higher conversion rates. For instance, e-commerce businesses such as REDCON1 have achieved a <a href="https://lifesight.io/blog/sms-marketing-for-ecommerce/#:~:text=Get%20inspired%20with%20growth%20insights,the%20value%20of%20SMS%20marketing.">high ROAS through SMS marketing</a>, with conversion rates ranging from 8% to 26% and low unsubscribe rates.<br></li><li><strong>Cost-effectiveness</strong>: Automated SMS campaigns require <a href="https://earthweb.com/blog/sms-marketing-statistics#:~:text=Automated%20SMS%20Requires%2060%25%20Less%20Human%20Intervention.">60% less human intervention</a> compared to traditional marketing strategies, making them a cost-effective solution.<br></li><li><strong>Enhanced customer experience</strong>: By delivering timely and relevant messages, SMS drip campaigns enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty. Studies show that<a href="https://financesonline.com/sms-marketing-statistics"> 75% of customers appreciate receiving special offers</a> via text messages.&nbsp;</li></ol><h2>6 SMS drip campaign examples</h2><p>Now, let’s focus on the most common use cases where you can plan an automated SMS drip campaign:&nbsp;</p><h3>1. Welcome series</h3><p>A welcome series SMS drip campaign is a series of automated messages planned for either a new customer or a potential customer who just subscribed to your SMS list. This series aims to make them more familiar with your brand, bestseller products, and potential offers and convince them to make a purchase.&nbsp;</p><p>With a well-crafted welcome series, you can make a positive first impression on your subscribers and set a base for how well they will engage with all future SMS campaigns.&nbsp;</p><figure style="max-width:853px" data-rt-max-width="853px"><div><img src="/images/blog/67fe0a58e92c61467c8f4ec8_675980cdf9a8635bf6237b72_AD_4nXeUuN-ULBAWZKCuGKVT28VcWg-ac148afa.png" alt="Welcome series SMS campaign example" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div><figcaption>Welcome series SMS campaign example</figcaption></figure><h3>2. Abandoned cart&nbsp;</h3><p>Another use case where an automated SMS campaign makes sense is cart abandonment. Abandoned cart SMS drip campaigns are automated text messages sent to customers who added items to their shopping cart but did not complete the purchase.&nbsp;</p><p>This campaign is critical because persuading an existing subscriber (who has expressed interest in your product) to make a purchase is easier than marketing to new consumers.&nbsp;</p><p>&nbsp;A common cart abandonment series can include any offer, low inventory alert, or a simple reminder.&nbsp;</p><figure style="max-width:853px" data-rt-max-width="853px"><div><img src="/images/blog/67fe0a58e92c61467c8f4ec5_675980ce0d2251f367c4c44b_AD_4nXetrRFgTOow6MNjqNDcXpBZif-9cc782f6.png" alt="Abandoned cart SMS campaign example" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div><figcaption>Abandoned cart SMS campaign example</figcaption></figure><h3>3. Customer feedback&nbsp;</h3><p>User-generated content is increasingly recognized as a pillar of modern marketing. It builds social proof and helps you build trust to generate more sales in the future.&nbsp;</p><p>One of the simplest ways to collect user-generated content is by running automated feedback campaigns.&nbsp;</p><p>Customer feedback SMS drip campaigns are automated text messages sent to users to provide feedback post-purchase. You can also offer any incentive to customers to give feedback.&nbsp;</p><figure style="max-width:861px" data-rt-max-width="861px"><div><img src="/images/blog/67fe0a58e92c61467c8f4ece_675980ce6d318f20200f86d8_AD_4nXceH_BZa-Pt0ZTBmKFt5l3qBo-bb64e246.png" alt="Customer feedback SMS campaign example" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div><figcaption>Customer feedback SMS campaign example</figcaption></figure><h3>4 Cross-sell and Upsell&nbsp;</h3><p>Cross-selling and upselling are powerful strategies to increase customer lifetime value. The goal is to recommend related or higher-value products that complement their last purchase.&nbsp;</p><p>You can define product bundles of SKUs that complement each other or identify bestsellers in a category. Whenever a user makes a purchase, they get these automated suggestions.&nbsp;</p><p>The intent should be to make a relevant recommendation rather than being spammy.</p><figure style="max-width:864px" data-rt-max-width="864px"><div><img src="/images/blog/67fe0a58e92c61467c8f4ec2_675980cdfb64f7b7e2f7567d_AD_4nXekrFQok6r4BV2dM0X0HUVFLH-a88b63fc.png" alt="Cross-sell and upsell SMS campaign example" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div><figcaption>Cross-sell and upsell SMS campaign example</figcaption></figure><h3>5. Winback</h3><p>Winback campaigns, also known as re-engagement or reactivation campaigns, are essential for long-term brand success.&nbsp;</p><p>As part of winback SMS drip campaigns, you can send messages to customers who haven't purchased in a while. The message could be a coupon, an announcement about the latest collection, or more. The idea is to regain their interest and give them enough incentive to make a purchase.&nbsp;</p><figure style="max-width:858px" data-rt-max-width="858px"><div><img src="/images/blog/67fe0a58e92c61467c8f4ed7_675980ce0327515ef089a404_AD_4nXff_R_TL-nx_6HDSMG7AapyTD-b235ba04.png" alt="Winback SMS campaign example" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div><figcaption>Winback SMS campaign example</figcaption></figure><h3>6. Loyalty program&nbsp;</h3><p>Loyalty programs are excellent for building long-term customer relationships. SMS is an excellent medium for running loyalty programs.&nbsp;</p><p>As part of the loyalty SMS drip campaigns, you can send messages about the rewards points, birthday rewards, milestones rewards, or any other benefits.&nbsp;</p><figure style="max-width:865px" data-rt-max-width="865px"><div><img src="/images/blog/67fe0a58e92c61467c8f4ecb_675980ced01fb6dbb2862acd_AD_4nXd4ceNf15a1_g9w8OOfpCvP2O-10ae0e70.png" alt="Loyalty program example" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div><figcaption>Loyalty program example</figcaption></figure><h2>How to create an SMS drip campaign?&nbsp;</h2><p>Follow these steps to create effective SMS drip campaigns:&nbsp;</p><h3>1.
