These emails aim to introduce the brand, set expectations, and provide relevant information or offers.&nbsp;</p><p>Example workflow:&nbsp;</p><ul><li>New customer signs up/makes a purchase</li><li>Send a welcome email with a discount code or incentive</li><li>If there is no purchase in 7 days, send a follow-up email with product recommendations</li><li>If purchased, move to a regular marketing sequence.</li></ul><h3>2. Abandoned cart emails&nbsp;</h3><figure><div><img src="/images/blog/67fe0a50b3415e5ee0b6d40d_6645f82d11d5d9517cf52767_yRM2NxQtYDAG4M7o2EmBOEVLYJZ3nx-9d7af3e5.gif" alt="" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div></figure><p>Abandoned cart emails Brief explanation: Abandoned cart emails are triggered when a customer adds items to their online shopping cart but doesn't complete the purchase. Order follow-up email&nbsp;</h3><figure><div><img src="/images/blog/67fe0a51b3415e5ee0b6d482_6645f82e14fafffa4798012f_EDjGVhlQOBxhZwAaqW5248l4T1e8iV-e8e404f6.png" alt="" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div></figure><p>Order follow-up emails are sent after a customer makes a purchase. These emails can include order confirmations, shipping updates, product care instructions, or cross-selling/upselling recommendations.&nbsp;</p><p>Example workflow:&nbsp;</p><ul><li>The customer makes a purchase&nbsp;</li><li>Send order confirmation email&nbsp;</li><li>When the order ships, send a shipping confirmation with the tracking number&nbsp;</li><li>Seven days after delivery, send a follow-up email with product care tips and related product recommendations.</li></ul><h3>4. Re-engagement campaigns</h3><figure><div><img src="/images/blog/67fe0a51b3415e5ee0b6d474_6645f840f19bb668cf7ee10e_kaNDAYRLfD7wMeFXgAl3Y7Uqc7cDtD-5c4585ff.png" alt="" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div></figure><p>Re-engagement campaigns aim to re-activate inactive subscribers or customers who haven't engaged with the brand for a certain period.&nbsp;</p><p>Example workflow:&nbsp;</p><ul><li>Subscriber hasn't opened or clicked an email in 90 days&nbsp;</li><li>Send a "We miss you" email with an incentive or content&nbsp;</li><li>If there is no engagement, send a second email with a stronger incentive or content&nbsp;</li><li>If there is still no engagement, move to a win-back campaign or remove it from the list</li></ul><h3>6.
