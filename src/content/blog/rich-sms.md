<p>For any brand to engage with high-value customers, rich SMS marketing remains a winning strategy. If you’re wondering why,&nbsp; the answer lies in your pocket or in your hand— it’s your smartphone. On&nbsp; average, a user checks their device <a href="https://www.validity.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/The-State-of-SMS-Marketing-in-2023.pdf">96 times a day</a>. For e-commerce brands looking to send text messages, that’s 96 opportunities to make a sale.&nbsp;</p><p>Interestingly, Grand View Research Inc.’s data shows that the U.S. SMS marketing market size is expected to reach <a href="https://www.grandviewresearch.com/press-release/us-sms-marketing-market-analysis">$9.96 billion by 2030</a>, expanding at a CAGR of 20.8%.</p><p>Clearly, traditional SMS marketing isn’t going anywhere anytime soon; however, rich SMS is really starting to take off as companies lean into apps like WhatsApp. Unlike standard SMS, which sticks to a 160-character limit, rich SMS gives you the freedom to share more detailed information.&nbsp;</p><p>We all know how tricky it can be to get your point across in just a few words, right? That’s why rich SMS is a much better option—it lets you link to landing pages where you can provide even more info.</p><p>Rich SMS helps companies run SMS campaigns that convert leads way better while also making the customer experience smoother with clear calls to action on those landing pages. With rich SMS, businesses can connect with consumers on a deeper level by creating an interactive experience.</p><p>In this blog post, we’ll explore what rich SMS marketing is, how it works, and how you can build a stellar campaign that can benefit your business.&nbsp;</p><h2>What is rich SMS marketing?</h2><p>Rich SMS is like the upgraded version of the classic SMS. It's much more practical because it can include a short clickable link that directs people to a specialized page. This link helps you get around the 150-character limit of regular SMS.</p><p>A rich SMS looks like a regular SMS but includes a hyperlink at the end. There are two types of hyperlinks you can add to rich SMS. First up, we have clean URLs. These are the classic links that you copy directly into the message, and they show up in full text for the recipient. Then there are short URLs, which are condensed versions of links. They help you save characters in your SMS, allowing you to say more with less.</p><p>So, what makes rich text messaging so special? It’s all about how it pairs with landing pages. This combo not only attracts and converts prospects but also builds customer loyalty and encourages actions like buying, renting, subscribing, registering, or filling a form.</p><p>For companies in the retail, e-commerce, and food delivery space, the combination of rich SMS and landing pages is a real game changer.</p><p>Plus, rich SMS can be packed with multimedia content, making the interaction between the sender and the recipient much more engaging. This could be anything from photos and videos to files and voice messages. This is called the rich communication services (RCS) message — another step forward from the traditional SMS that helps businesses get closer to their customers and prospects.</p><p>Think of RCS messages as the Android equivalent of Apple’s iMessage. They blend the best features of messaging apps like WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and WeChat right into the SMS format.&nbsp;</p><h2>How a successful rich SMS marketing campaign can benefit your business&nbsp;</h2><p><a href="https://www.validity.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/The-State-of-SMS-Marketing-in-2023.pdf">Fifty-two percent of consumers </a>have purchased products and 38% have opted for services after receiving brand messages via text/SMS or other messaging platform. Better engagement with your products and services&nbsp;</h3><p>As per <a href="https://developers.google.com/static/business-communications/rcs-business-messaging/files/rcs-business-messaging-1-pager.pdf">Google Data</a>,&nbsp; 74% of individuals are likely to engage with a brand using RCS. Personalized approach to boost brand loyalty</h3><p>With rich SMS marketing, you can tailor messages for each customer based on their individual interests and behaviors. Drive traffic to other platforms like websites/apps.</h3><p>You can incorporate rich SMS into a more comprehensive marketing plan. It can be integrated with social media, email campaigns, and other channels to create a unified marketing campaign that maximizes impact and reach.</p><h2>How rich SMS works&nbsp;</h2><p>Rich SMS is a great way to not only attract new customers but also retain existing ones. Let’s explore a few examples to show you how all of this works.</p><p>You’re an e-commerce business wanting to connect with your customers soon after they make a purchase. Sending a rich SMS the day after they buy your product or service is a prime opportunity to strengthen your relationship with them.
