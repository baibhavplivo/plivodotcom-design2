Others focus on building a strong infrastructure to manage domestic OTP deliveries.&nbsp;</p><p>OTP providers also vary in the availability and flexibility of APIs and SDKs for integration into existing systems, impacting the ability to customize and implement the OTP experience in your existing tech stack.&nbsp;</p><p>Choosing the right OTP provider requires defining your requirements and goals, vetting various providers, and understanding how well each solution works with your existing tech stack.&nbsp;</p><p>In this guide, we’ll explain the features you need to look for in an SMS OTP service provider, the top seven most popular SMS OTP service providers in the market, and how to choose the right one for your needs.&nbsp;</p><h2>What features should I look for in an SMS OTP service provider?</h2><p>Consider eight features when choosing an SMS OTP service provider.&nbsp;</p><ul><li>Time-to-deploy&nbsp;</li><li>SMS OTP conversion rates</li><li>Fraud detection</li><li>Security and compliance</li><li>Analytics and tracking</li><li>Integrations</li><li>Customer support</li><li>Price</li></ul><h3>1. Time-to-deploy&nbsp;</h3><p>Developers already have enough on their plates— and adding a complicated tool t that requires intensive onboarding and implementation is counterproductive to achieving your other development needs.&nbsp;</p><p>When selecting an OTP provider, look for services that require minimal setup. This will allow your team to start sending OTPs with little to no downtime.&nbsp;</p><p>Your SMS OTP service provider should also provide clear documentation and SDKs that make it straightforward for developers to integrate OTP with your platforms, significantly reducing the time and resources needed for deployment.</p><h3>2. SMS OTP conversion rates&nbsp;</h3><p>One of the most frustrating experiences a customer can have is receiving a delayed OTP, or worse, not receiving an OTP at all. Delayed or undelivered OTPs can cause anxiety, potentially leading the customer to abandon the transaction or lose trust in your brand.&nbsp;</p><p>Verify that an SMS OTP provider has high delivery rates to ensure that your customers can receive and act upon OTP messages.</p><h3>3. Fraud detection</h3><p>In international markets, OTPs in SMS and voice channels are particularly susceptible to SMS Pumping fraud.&nbsp;</p><p>Advanced fraud detection should include dynamic risk assessment algorithms that adapt to emerging threats and unusual patterns.&nbsp;</p><h3>4. Security and compliance</h3><p>When assessing the security and compliance of an OTP service provider, there are two key aspects you need to consider: data encryption and regulatory compliance.&nbsp;</p><p>End-to-end encryption ensures that an OTP is encrypted at the origin and only decrypts at the receiver’s device. This protects the OTP message from being intercepted or read by hackers. To verify the robustness of the encryption, look for an OTP service provider that uses recognized standards like AES (Advanced Encryption Standard) with a suitable key length (e.g., 256-bit).&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><p>Additionally, look for OTP service providers that adhere to data protection regulations like the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) as they are mandatory for different countries.&nbsp;</p><h3>5. Analytics and tracking</h3><p>Choose an SMS OTP service provider that offers detailed analytics and tracking. Real-time insights into OTP delivery rates and failures help identify issues as they arise.&nbsp;</p><p>Custom reports with a trend analysis of KPIs, such as success rates of OTP deliveries across different regions, can highlight areas for improvement, whether in technology or carrier partnerships. You can also leverage these reports to make strategic decisions and draw behavioral insights.&nbsp;</p><h3>6. Integrations</h3><p>Even the most robust OTP provider must play well with your existing tech stack. Think about which platforms you need your OTP tool to communicate with. Does this OTP solution fit your long-term technological needs?&nbsp;</p><p><a href="https://www.plivo.com/">Plivo</a>, for example, integrates with <a href="https://www.plivo.com/docs/integrations/">most popular tools</a> including Zapier, Microsoft Power Automate, Integromat, Pipedream, Integrately, GitHub Actions, EngageBay, and more.&nbsp;</p><h3>7. Customer support</h3><p>Look for an SMS OTP service provider that is available 24/7. This level or service translates to immediate assistance, minimized downtime, and enhanced reliability.
