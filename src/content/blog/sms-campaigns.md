<p>You’ve spent time crafting emails that end up in spam, social posts lost in crowded feeds, and costly ad campaigns that aren’t sustainable.&nbsp;</p><p>The real problem? Your message isn’t reaching customers where it matters most.</p><p>Now, imagine a solution that bypasses these barriers and connects with your audience directly&nbsp; in the palm of their hand. That’s the power of SMS marketing campaigns.&nbsp;</p><p>With the average person spending <a href="https://explodingtopics.com/blog/smartphone-usage-stats">almost 4.5 hours a day</a> on their phone, SMS or text marketing&nbsp; campaigns have a higher&nbsp; chance of&nbsp; engaging customers and driving sales.</p><p>In this blog post, we dive into&nbsp; SMS campaigns, share examples of successful texting campaigns, and reveal&nbsp; top tips to create effective text marketing&nbsp; campaigns.&nbsp;</p><h2>What are SMS campaigns?</h2><p>SMS campaigns are a marketing strategy that sends targeted text messages to promote services and products, or convey information&nbsp; to a targeted list of recipients.&nbsp;</p><p>Text messages are brief, so even a quick glance typically means your audience has read the entire message. You can effectively convey your message almost every time.</p><p>These texting campaigns are typically used by businesses to directly communicate with customers. SMS campaigns boast&nbsp; high open rates and are perfect&nbsp; for:</p><ul><li>Promoting sales and special offers</li><li>Announcing product launches</li><li>Sending order and shipping updates</li><li>Delivering&nbsp; time-sensitive alerts and reminders</li><li>Building customer loyalty with personalized messages</li></ul><h3>Benefits of text marketing campaigns for e-commerce</h3><p><strong>High engagement rates</strong></p><p>With <a href="https://www.forbes.com/councils/theyec/2024/02/05/is-sms-marketing-an-affordable-small-business-growth-booster/">open rates as high as 98%</a>, SMS campaigns can increase customer engagement. Also, most people check their text messages within minutes of receiving them, so your message is almost guaranteed to be seen quickly.</p><p><strong>Personalization opportunities</strong></p><p>By using customer data in your SMS campaigns—such as their name, past purchases, or browsing history—you can tailor your SMS campaigns to each individual recipient.</p><p><strong>Direct and immediate communication</strong></p><p>SMS messages are perfect for creating a sense of urgency, for example, “Sale ends today” or “Limited stock available.” Texts are delivered instantly and customers are more likely to take prompt action.</p><p><strong>Customer retention</strong></p><p>Discount coupons and offers aren’t the only way to retain customers or to get them to subscribe for SMS marketing campaigns. But they do play a big role.&nbsp;</p><p>According to a Forbes piece, <a href="https://www.forbes.com/sites/square/2021/02/16/the-art-of-discounting-what-works-now-what-doesnt/?sh=272ab6967fcc">77% of consumers</a> say discounts influence where they shop.</p><p>This means that customers are choosing to purchase through SMS. If you maintain the <a href="https://www.plivo.com/cx/blog/sms-marketing-best-practices">best SMS marketing practices</a>, you can retain more customers.&nbsp;</p><p><strong>Integration with Shopify and e-commerce tools</strong></p><p>Most SMS marketing tools integrate with your Shopify store. You can <a href="https://www.plivo.com/cx/blog/top-sms-marketing-apps-for-shopify">sync Shopify contacts</a> and collect data from your storefront.&nbsp;</p><p>This builds an automated system where you don’t have to switch multiple platforms to manage campaigns and see results.
