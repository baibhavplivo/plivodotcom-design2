Passthrough fees for outbound messages vary depending on the carrier network used to deliver the messages. Your <a href="/glossary/what-is-sms-verification" target="_blank">SMS verification API</a> partner adds these fees to the per-message rate.</p><p>Think of this like a sales tax — every provider charges the fee based on what each carrier charges them.
