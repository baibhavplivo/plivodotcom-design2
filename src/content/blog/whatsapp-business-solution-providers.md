<p>As your business scales, transitioning from the WhatsApp business app to the WhatsApp Business Platform will boost customer engagement and streamline operations. The platform’s automated messaging feature, easy CRM integration, and advanced analytics support these goals.&nbsp;</p><p>The right WhatsApp business solution partner offers advanced analytics designed to turn customer interactions into insights. Deeper customer insights, such as the best time to engage or messaging preferences, can help you overcome low engagement or customer turnover.&nbsp;</p><p>Whether you’re moving from the WhatsApp business app to the Business Platform, or searching for a new Business Service Provider (BSP), this <a href="https://www.plivo.com/blog/whatsapp-business-api-guide/">WhatsApp API business guide</a> will help you navigate these decisions.</p><p>We’ll discuss how to select the right WhatsApp BSP, explore its benefits and key features, and provide a detailed review of the top six <a href="https://www.plivo.com/whatsapp/">WhatsApp API</a> providers on the market, along with the cost considerations.</p><h2>What is a WhatsApp business solution partner?</h2><p>A <a href="https://faq.whatsapp.com/695500918177858/">WhatsApp business solution partner</a> (BSP) is a third-party company approved by WhatsApp to help other businesses access the WhatsApp Business API.&nbsp;</p><p>These providers integrate WhatsApp business messaging into your existing business communication tools. They handle all the pre and post-WhatsApp business integration challenges, freeing your marketing team to focus on improving customer engagement.&nbsp;</p><h2>Why does WhatsApp work with partners?</h2><p>Meta’s vision is to make WhatsApp conversations the primary way businesses and consumers connect. WhatsApp business solution partners shape this vision. These partners assist businesses in planning, building, and implementing effective solutions on the WhatsApp Business platform.&nbsp;</p><p>They offer comprehensive services including:</p><ul><li>Driving awareness of WhatsApp business platform solutions</li><li>Integration services</li><li>Chatbot integration</li><li>Strong customer support</li></ul><p>With <a href="https://business.whatsapp.com/">over a billion users</a> engaging via WhatsApp each week, this channel is quickly becoming a core component of marketing and customer service for companies worldwide.</p><h2>How does a WhatsApp business solution partner work?</h2><p>A WhatsApp business solution partner does all the legwork for managing conversations between businesses and their audience on WhatsApp.&nbsp;</p><p>Here’s how they work.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><ol><li><strong>Access to the WhatsApp Business API: </strong>These providers give you access to the WhatsApp Business API to scale your messaging strategy.&nbsp;</li><li><strong>Technical setup</strong>: They ensure perfect API integration with your existing business communication tools or customer experience tools such as a CRM or&nbsp; ERP.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</li><li><strong>Account onboarding</strong>: They <a href="https://www.plivo.com/blog/get-started-with-whatsapp-api/">onboard your WhatsApp Business account</a>, and take care of all necessary verifications and approvals required by WhatsApp.&nbsp;</li><li><strong>Automate workflows</strong>: WhatsApp business solution partners set up automated workflows that streamline recurring tasks, such as order confirmations and appointment reminders, so you can focus on high-value tasks.&nbsp;</li></ol><p>Plus, most WhatsApp business solution partners offer customizable templates, ongoing technical support, and comprehensive training resources to keep your messaging strategy running smoothly.&nbsp;</p><p>The level and quality of support varies between providers: consider your requirements and choose a provider that closely aligns with them.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><h2>Benefits of using an official WhatsApp business service provider</h2><p>Companies that work with an official WhatsApp business solution partner start seeing immediate benefits, such as:&nbsp;</p><ul><li>Direct access to call WhatsApp Business platform&nbsp;</li><li>Permission to perform actions on behalf of a business, such as sending and receiving messages, managing templates, etc.&nbsp;</li><li>Onboard businesses using Embedded Signup&nbsp;</li><li>Eligible for partner program incentives&nbsp;</li><li>Meet with Meta’s messaging, <a href="https://www.facebook.com/policies_center/commerce">commerce</a>, and compliance policies</li><li>Manage billing on behalf of a business with a single line of credit sharing</li></ul><p>Providers offer more than just these basic features to help businesses get the most out of the WhatsApp Business Platform.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p><h3><strong>Reach customers at scale&nbsp;</strong></h3><p>If scalability is your goal, choose Plivo as your WhatsApp business solution partner. Look for a provider with a track record of reliable communication.&nbsp;</p><h3><strong>Address common queries with automated messaging</strong></h3><p>Engaging a growing customer base is no easy feat. Automated messaging can facilitate customer engagement at scale.&nbsp; Look for providers that offer pre-built solutions and templates to streamline the process of sending automated notifications, promotional messages, and interactive content.&nbsp;</p><h3><strong>Get a thorough WhatsApp marketing campaign analysis</strong></h3><p>Say you’re testing a new WhatsApp marketing campaign on two target groups. You want to measure the performance of both. How do you know which one’s working? A WhatsApp business service provider grants access to detailed metrics and analytics. These valuable insights into customer interactions give your business a competitive edge.&nbsp;</p><p><strong><em>Note: </em></strong><em>While BSPs handle most of the technical aspects, Meta’s recent policy shift requires some involvement from you. This process typically takes less than a week.</em></p><h2>How to Choose a WhatsApp business solution partner&nbsp;</h2><p>Start by exploring the <a href="https://en-gb.facebook.com/business/partner-directory/search?solution_type=campaign_management&amp;ref=pd_home_hero_cta">Meta’s partner directory</a>, where you can filter the partners based on the specific solution you’re looking for or even search by partner name.&nbsp;</p><p>If you’re unsure which WhatsApp business solution partner is right for you, start with these key questions:&nbsp;</p><ul><li>What is your current and projected message volume?&nbsp;&nbsp;</li><li>Does your business require automation for frequently asked questions?&nbsp;</li><li>What’s your budget?&nbsp;</li><li>What level of customer support does the WhatsApp business solution partner offer?
