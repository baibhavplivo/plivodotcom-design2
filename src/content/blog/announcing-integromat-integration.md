<p><a href="https://www.integromat.com/?ref=plivo-release-notes">Integromat</a>, a tool for automating workflows, lets nontechnical people automate connections between applications, services, and devices to transfer and transform data without programming. Integromat has added support for Plivo.</p><p>We’re written a <a href="https://www.plivo.com/docs/integrations/integromat/getting-started/">getting started guide</a> to walk you through what you need to know to use Plivo with Integromat. We also wrote a <a href="https://www.plivo.com/docs/integrations/integromat/live-poll-with-google-sheets/">live poll application</a> that shows, step by step, how to integrate two applications — Plivo and Google Sheets — as an example.</p><p></p>
