Once you integrate Intercom into your workflow, there are these key factors that come into play:</p><h4>1.Fin AI Agent-driven resolutions</h4><p>As noted above, if you have installed the Fin AI Agent chatbot, you are charged $0.99 monthly for each resolution.</p><h4>2.People reached</h4><p>This calculates the total number of unique customers you've contacted across all your communication channels. It essentially counts every customer you've sent a message to, regardless of the channel.</p><p>That means if you sent 100 emails and 50 bounced, you’ll still be charged for the 100 people reached. It also considers people who have received and engaged with an outbound message.&nbsp;</p><h4>3.Seats<br></h4><ul><li><strong>Full seats:</strong> Each teammate using Intercom must have a designated full seat, which provides access to products and features within your chosen plan.<br><br></li><li><strong>Lite seats:</strong> Included in Advanced and Expert plans, Lite seats offer limited access to Intercom for cross-functional collaboration at no extra cost. The Essential plan does not include Lite seats.<br><br></li><li><strong>Additional seats</strong>: Each additional agent seat is charged extra based on your subscription plan. Each additional seat on the Essential plan may be added for $39/month, $99/month for the Advanced plan and $139/month for the Expert plan.</li></ul><h3>Intercom pricing: Additional costs</h3><p>To enjoy Intercom to the fullest, you have to pay for a set of additional costs:</p><h4>1.WhatsApp</h4><p>Intercom has different pricing for inbound and outbound WhatsApp and is charged per ‘conversation.’ The unit costs vary by volume.</p><h4>2.SMS</h4><p>SMS is charged per the ‘SMS sent and/or received’ number. The unit cost and prices may vary by volume and region, respectively.</p><h4>3.Emails and email Campaigns</h4><p>All email campaigns are charged per the number of ‘bulk emails sent’ — emails sent to more than one customer. Emails sent from the inbox or the inbound emails from your customers and responses to those emails are included in your plan at no additional charge.</p><h4>4.Proactive Support Plus &amp; Messages Sent</h4><p>If you purchase Proactive Support Plus, you can access several capabilities, including product tours, surveys, in-app posts, mobile push, and mobile carousels.&nbsp;</p><p>The base fee is $99/month for 500 messages sent monthly.
