<p>With customer expectations and demands constantly soaring, AI chatbots can be your sales teams’ strongest partners. Powered with the latest tech, they serve as round-the-clock virtual agents who are always online, ready to field queries, convert leads and, most importantly, reduce customer support costs by <a href="https://www.ibm.com/blogs/watson/2017/10/how-chatbots-reduce-customer-service-costs-by-30-percent/">up to 30%</a>.&nbsp;</p><p>Invesp reports that chatbots have significantly enhanced customer service efficiency. They can handle <a href="https://adamconnell.me/chatbot-statistics/">80% of routine inquiries</a> while delivering responses 80% faster than human agents. This remarkable speed translates into substantial time savings, as <a href="https://www.juniperresearch.com/press/press-releases/chatbots-to-deliver-11bn-cost-savings-2023">Juniper Research predicts</a> chatbots will save businesses and consumers a combined 2.5 billion hours.</p><p>Since chatbots are often the first point of contact for inbound prospects, they must be used strategically. These tools, combined with the intuitive capabilities of AI, can help close deals and raise revenue efficiently, all while allowing you to scale sales teams’ operations.</p><p>In this blog post, we will discuss how an AI sales bot fits right into your sales tech stack and how you can use different AI chatbots to increase sales conversions.&nbsp;</p><h2>What is an AI sales bot?</h2><p>An AI sales bot is a software automation tool that leverages natural language processing (NLP), machine learning, and knowledge base integration to simulate natural, human-like conversations with customers.&nbsp;</p><p>Acting as a virtual agent, an AI sales bot intuitively responds to customer queries, provides information about the product, and helps customers self-serve in their interactions with your business. These tools proactively offer assistance to prospects from the get-go, taking them through the entire sales process down to the post-purchase phase—without little to no intervention from the sales teams.&nbsp;</p><p>AI sales bots are quite versatile and can be classified into the following types:&nbsp;</p><ul><li><strong>Knowledge-based chatbots&nbsp;</strong></li></ul><p>A knowledge-based chatbot responds to customer queries and FAQs by searching through your knowledge base and surfacing the relevant articles.</p><ul><li><strong>Voice bots&nbsp;</strong></li></ul><p>Voice bots are advanced AI-based software that uses spoken language for voice interactions. It greets and guides inbound callers, executes customer requests, and routes the call to an agent.</p><ul><li><strong>Decision tree chatbots&nbsp;</strong></li></ul><p>A decision-tree chatbot, also known as a rules-based chatbot, provides pre-programmed responses to customer queries without the involvement of a live agent. It maps out conversations in a flow chart format, predicting customer queries and programming the responses accordingly.&nbsp;</p><ul><li><strong>Task-specific chatbots</strong></li></ul><p>Task-specific chatbots are built with the capability to perform certain tasks for customers, like setting up customer accounts, taking users through purchases, and connecting customers to a live agent.&nbsp;</p><h2>What are the benefits of using AI sales bots?&nbsp;</h2><p>Incorporating an AI chatbot into your sales efforts comes with the following benefits:&nbsp;</p><h3>Generate leads efficiently</h3><p>Many high-intent buyers on their way to making a purchase may have certain questions and queries about the product that the AI sales bot can rapidly address. Besides responding to queries, these bots can analyze a range of interactions to identify potential leads. More sophisticated sales bots also enable lead qualification, helping sales teams spring into action and zero in on the hot leads.&nbsp;</p><h3>Round-the-clock availability&nbsp;</h3><p>Sales bots are built to field queries from potential customers, irrespective of the business hours or the peak holiday season. They cater to markets across time zones and geographies, ensuring that none of your precious leads and customers slip through the cracks.&nbsp;</p><h3>Scale-up with minimal costs&nbsp;</h3><p>AI chatbots reduce the dependency on human agents for simple, repetitive tasks. So, when expanding your business, you can count on your AI sales bot to perform various sales tasks like answering FAQs, sharing product details, and routing leads to the right knowledge base—eliminating the need to hire new human agents.&nbsp;</p><h3>Automate support&nbsp;</h3><p>Sales bots can go beyond fielding queries with <a href="https://www.plivo.com/cx/blog/customer-service-automation">customer support automation</a>. Knowledge-based chatbots are designed to direct customers to relevant articles and resources to help solve their queries.&nbsp;</p><p>By incorporating automation into your sales processes, AI sales bots can notably reduce the workload of your sales agents.</p><h3>Upselling opportunities</h3><p>The probability of selling to an <a href="https://www.invespcro.com/blog/customer-acquisition-retention/#:~:text=The%20probability%20of%20selling%20to,when%20compared%20to%20new%20customers.">existing customer is 60-70%</a>, compared to the probability of selling to a new prospect. Sales bots can send premium products or service recommendations to customers based on their transaction history.&nbsp;</p><h3>Customer sentiment analytics&nbsp;</h3><p>Many AI sales bots use machine learning and natural language processing to capture customer sentiment. This gives agents clear cues on framing responses that are best in tune with the customers’ moods.&nbsp;</p><h3>Integration with analytics&nbsp;</h3><p>Tracking chatbot performance is essential for improving the overall customer experience, monitoring the number of leads generated, and determining the average time spent per conversation.&nbsp;</p><p>Seeing prospects drop off during chats? Chatbot analytics can also reveal when and why that’s happening.&nbsp;</p><p>‍</p><h2>AI sales bots in action: Top use cases&nbsp;</h2><p>Incorporating AI sales bots into your sales operations is a smart and strategic move that will enhance your overall <a href="https://www.plivo.com/cx/service">customer service experience</a>. Let’s explore the most impactful use cases where bots can deliver the most value:&nbsp;</p><h3>Discounts and offers&nbsp;</h3><p>One of the most effective ways sales bots can generate more revenue is by surfacing timely discounts and offers. For instance, sales chatbots can pop up on specific categories and product pages, offering a targeted discount based on customer browsing patterns and purchase history.&nbsp;</p><h3>Winning back abandoned carts</h3><p>Cart abandonment can be a huge challenge, increasing the loss potential and inventory confusion. In such situations, the sales bot can encourage purchases by dropping automated follow-up messages reminding customers about the items left behind in the cart.&nbsp;</p><h3>Answering FAQs</h3><p>An AI sales bot can be at prospects' beck and call, responding to their questions and directing them to the integrated knowledge base resources and articles. This ensures that the leads’ queries are acknowledged and addressed on time.</p><h3>Helpdesk and automated ticketing</h3><p>Sales chatbots can also help streamline helpdesk operations and ticketing systems. For instance, when a customer encounters an app crash, they can initiate a chat with the AI bot and share details about the issue. Based on these details, the chatbot routes the ticket automatically to the corresponding tech support.</p><h3>Dynamic pricing adjustments</h3><p>Also known as surge pricing or demand pricing, dynamic pricing adjustments allow e-commerce retailers to adjust product and service prices flexibly based on market trends. AI-powered chatbots can adjust dynamic pricing by tapping into vital customer data such as demand, customer behavior, and market conditions.</p><h3>Lead scoring and prioritization</h3><p>AI sales bots play a crucial role in identifying promising leads/accounts. Using <a href="https://www.plivo.com/cx/blog/conversational-ai-for-customer-service">conversational AI</a>, the chatbot analyzes customer interactions, behaviors, and engagement to assign different scores to different leads.&nbsp;</p><p>‍</p><h2>Best AI sales bots in the market right now</h2><p>Integrating AI sales bots into your communications is not just desirable but a must-have for building productive sales processes.
