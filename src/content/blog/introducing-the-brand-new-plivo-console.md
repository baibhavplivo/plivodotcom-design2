<p>Today we’re unveiling a new, redesigned <a href="https://console.plivo.com/">Plivo console</a>. The main objectives for the redesign were to organize account information better and make it easier to manage it, and to create a scalable user interface to accommodate future products and features.</p><p>We achieved these goals by introducing sections for products, accounts, and billing.</p><h2>Product sections</h2><p>The new console has a section for each product. Features and items relevant to a product are available in the menu sidebars on the left of each console page.</p><figure style="max-width:1025px" data-rt-max-width="1025px"><div><img src="/images/blog/6583d0276bdb2b6e89232ee0_console-screen-shot-01.jpeg" alt="Console Screen Shot" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div></figure><h2>Accounts and billing sections</h2><p>You can access accounts and billing pages from the settings menu in the top right corner.</p><figure style="max-width:1025px" data-rt-max-width="1025px"><div><img src="/images/blog/6583d0270c010c5de0a30825_console-screen-shot-02.png" alt="Console Screen Shot 02" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div></figure><p>The accounts section provides controls on account settings and subaccounts, and the billing section allows you to manage recharges, recharge options, usage bills, and payment receipts.</p><p>You can use the new <a href="https://console.plivo.com/">Plivo console</a> to perform all the tasks and actions available on the existing Plivo dashboard. <a href="https://console.plivo.com/accounts/login/">Log in</a> and let us know what you think.</p>
