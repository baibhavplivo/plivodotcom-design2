<p><a href="https://www.engagebay.com/">EngageBay</a> is an affordable all-in-one marketing, sales, and support CRM platform for small businesses. By integrating Plivo with EngageBay you can send SMS messages and make calls to your EngageBay contacts.</p><p>To use Plivo with EngageBay, you’ll need an <a href="https://app.engagebay.com/signup">EngageBay account</a> and a voice- and SMS-enabled Plivo phone number. You can purchase numbers from the <a href="https://console.plivo.com/active-phone-numbers/">Phone Numbers</a> page of the Plivo console, or by using the <a href="https://www.plivo.com/docs/numbers/">PhoneNumber API</a>.</p><figure><div><img src="/images/blog/657ff4d6327757ab84f16830_buy-new-number.png" alt="Buy a New Plivo Number" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div></figure><h2>Set up Plivo in EngageBay</h2><p>To get started, log in to the EngageBay dashboard, click on the Profile drop-down at the top right, then Preferences &gt; Gadgets &gt; Plivo (click <strong>Enable</strong>). </p><figure><div><img src="/images/blog/657ff4d67546e59809749ab7_setup-plivo-step1.png" alt="Enable Plivo integration on Engagebay" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div></figure><p>Enter your Plivo Auth ID and Auth Token, which you can find on the overview page of the <a href="https://console.plivo.com/dashboard/">Plivo console</a>, and click <strong>Validate</strong>. </p><figure><div><img src="/images/blog/657ff4d61efcac0bf1dc2d60_setup-plivo-step2.png" alt="Configure your Plivo auth ID and auth token" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div></figure><p>Once you validate your credentials, the next screen will prompt you to choose the Plivo phone numbers you want to enable on EngageBay. Choose the options you want and click <strong>Save</strong>. </p><figure><div><img src="/images/blog/657ff4d6b024190af123e3f1_setup-plivo-step3.png" alt="Configure other options" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div></figure><p>Now you can use your Plivo number within EngageBay to send SMS messages and make voice calls.</p><h2>Send SMS messages using Plivo</h2><p>To send a text message, navigate to the contact you want to send a message to. Under the phone number in the left pane click <strong>SMS</strong>, then select Plivo from the drop-down menu. </p><figure><div><img src="/images/blog/657ff4d61e8318870c6d1f56_sendsms-step1.png" alt="Navigate to your contact list and send SMS" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div></figure><p>A pop-up appears that prompts for the From number and message text you want to use. Fill in the details, then click <strong>Send</strong>. EngageBay will send the message to the contact’s phone number using Plivo as the SMS provider in the background. </p><figure><div><img src="/images/blog/657ff4d6a175ab06e0cdc14f_sendsms-step2.png" alt="Choose your Plivo number as Source number and fill the message body" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div></figure><h2>Make calls using Plivo</h2><p>To make a call, navigate to the contact you want to call to. Under the phone number in the left pane click <strong>Call</strong>, then select Plivo from the drop-down menu. </p><figure><div><img src="/images/blog/657ff4d6145e6b58c965f302_makecalls-step1.png" alt="Navigate to your contact list and make calls" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div></figure><p>You’ll be asked to choose one of the Plivo numbers as the caller ID for the call. Once you select the phone number, EngageBay will call the contact’s phone number using Plivo as the voice provider in the background. </p><figure><div><img src="/images/blog/657ff4d61650bb250fc5dc33_makecalls-step2.png" alt="Choose your Plivo number as the caller ID for the call" width="auto" height="auto" loading="auto"></div></figure><h2>More Plivo integrations</h2><p>You can integrate Plivo with many other integrations using platforms such as <a href="https://www.plivo.com/docs/integrations/zapier/">Zapier</a> and <a href="https://www.plivo.com/docs/integrations/microsoft-power-automate/">Microsoft Power Automate</a>. If your company has a SaaS platform and you’d like to integrate Plivo with it so you can give your customers easy access to voice calling and text messaging, <a href="mailto:integrations@plivo.com">let us know</a>.<br></p><p></p>
