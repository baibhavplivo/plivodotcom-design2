<p>Modern customers expect support across multiple channels—emails, live chat, social media, SMS, you name it.&nbsp;</p><p>Omnichannel contact center software unifies multiple communication channels, empowers your customer service team with 360 context, automation and insights, and helps you build stronger customer relationships.</p><p>But with so many options available, choosing the right tool might feel like finding a needle in a haystack.</p><p>This post reviews the top five omnichannel contact center platforms to help you find the perfect fit for your unique needs and budget.&nbsp;</p><h2>What is Omnichannel Contact Center Software?</h2><p>Omnichannel contact center software is a cloud-based communication hub for businesses. It helps you manage all your customer interactions across multiple channels, like phone calls, emails, texts, and social media messages, all in one place.</p><p>What makes an omnichannel contact center irreplaceable for modern and internet-first businesses is that conversational data and context from multiple channels synchronize automatically. A customer can start a chat, then decide to email about it or hop on a call if needed, all within the same conversation.</p><p>All the conversation history, regardless of the channel used, is logged and readily accessible to your agents without the need to search through multiple applications.&nbsp;</p><h2>Why Do You Need Omnichannel Contact Center Software?</h2><p>You need an omnichannel contact center to provide better customer service, offer an improved agent experience, allow remote work, and get insights into how your customer support team is performing.</p><h3>To provide better customer service</h3><p>Imagine yourself in the shoes of a customer, frantically searching for an answer online, then waiting on hold for ages on the phone, only to be directed to email. It’s a huge turn-off.&nbsp;</p><p><a href="https://www.salesforce.com/resources/research-reports/state-of-the-connected-customer/">79% of customers</a> today expect seamless, effortless support across channels, with the primary cause of their frustration being disjointed interactions.</p><p>Omnichannel software eliminates this problem by allowing your customer support team to be available on all in-demand communication channels. The holistic view of each customer's history and interactions allows support agents to diagnose and resolve issues swiftly, leading to satisfied customers.&nbsp;</p><h3>To offer a better agent experience</h3><p>When contact center reps juggle through multiple calls, emails, and chats simultaneously, it's stressful, inefficient, and hurts both agent morale and customer service.&nbsp;</p><p><a href="https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20200602005422/en/Improving-Agent-Experience-is-Essential-for-Improving-Customer-Satisfaction-and-Loyalty">30% of contact center staff</a> believe inadequate or difficult-to-use systems are the biggest obstacle in assisting customers. It's hard to understand customer journeys from scattered information with missing context or from conversations across disjointed channels.&nbsp;</p><p>Omnichannel software brings all customer interactions onto a single platform, giving customer support agents a holistic view of customer journeys and helping them resolve issues more efficiently.</p><p>Advanced tools also automate tasks like routing inquiries to the right agent, assigning priorities, and managing queues. Automation frees up valuable time for agents to focus on what matters most—providing exceptional service.</p><h3>To allow your customer service team to work remotely</h3><p>Most omnichannel software is cloud-based, meaning agents can work remotely using their own devices. Remote working not only improves agent experience but also reduces your operational costs.&nbsp;</p><p>You also get a productivity boost—according to Forbes, <a href="https://www.forbes.com/advisor/business/remote-work-statistics/">35% of remote employees</a> report increased productivity.&nbsp;</p><p>The cherry on top? A geographically dispersed team means that you can offer round-the-clock customer support.&nbsp;</p><h3>To get insights into the performance of your customer service team</h3><p>Omnichannel software provides built-in reporting tools that track key performance indicators (KPIs) like first contact resolution rates, average handling times, and customer satisfaction scores.&nbsp;</p><p>With quantifiable data, analyze trends, identify common issues, and understand how your customer support team performs across different channels.&nbsp;</p><p>This helps you look for areas for improvement, make data-driven decisions, and implement targeted training programs and development opportunities to help each agent reach their full potential.</p><h2>What are the Best Omnichannel Contact Center Tools?</h2><p>Omnichannel contact center software has many benefits, but only if you choose the right tool.
